
Left Click		>	Place Item/Tile
Shift + Left Click	>	Place Item/Tile behind all other Items/Tiles (note: items are always ontop of tiles)
Alt + Left CLick	>	Choose defaultTile (background)

Right Click		>	Remove object
Alt + Right Click	>	Remove defaultTile (background)

W, A, S, D		>	Pan the camera. This moves your view of the map around.

R or SCROLL_UP		>	Zoom in

F or SCROLL_DOWN	>	Zoom out

T			>	Test Map in game-play mode

Q, Middle Click		>	Pick object

Esc			>	Exit map maker. You will return to the previous screen.

Del			>	Clears the map of all items and tiles

Control + z		>	undo last tile/item placed
